    3. Remove double spaces : 'X    WATER (' -> 'X WATER ('
    4. Remove odd characters at the end. : 'X WATER (' -> 'X WATER '
    5. Trim : 'X WATER (' -> 'X WATER'
    6. Word Replacements / Synonyms
  2. Tokenize ([space], [(], [.] not followed by number or character) : 'X WATER' ->
    X
    WATER
  3. Translate
